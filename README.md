Space Shooter

Space Shooter is an arcade-style survival game where the player controls a spaceship and must defend against waves of incoming enemy ships. The objective is to survive each wave, manage resources, and defeat the final boss.

Enemy ships continuously appear in waves and attempt to destroy the player by firing their own weapons. The player must dodge attacks and shoot lasers to eliminate enemies before they overwhelm the battlefield.

Throughout the game, power-ups randomly appear to help the player survive longer. These buffs can restore health, replenish ammo, provide temporary shields, or boost the ship’s speed. Some special buffs also grant unique laser abilities, such as firing multiple shots at once or launching guided projectiles that track enemy ships.

If the player successfully survives all waves, a powerful boss enemy appears. Defeating the boss results in victory, while losing all three lives results in game over.

Key Features

Wave-Based Gameplay – Survive multiple waves of enemy ships.

Player Combat System – Shoot lasers while avoiding enemy attacks.

Three-Life System – The game ends when all lives are lost.

Random Buffs – Power-ups appear during gameplay to assist the player.

Multiple Power-Up Types

Health restoration

Temporary shield

Ammo refill

Speed boost

Special Weapon Buffs

Triple-shot laser

Homing laser that tracks enemies

Boss Battle – Defeat the final boss after surviving all waves.
